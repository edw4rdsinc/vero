Frequently Asked Questions

Have I used your team before?

Possibly! Many of us served Portland with another auto glass company before starting Vero. We're the same experienced technicians, now operating under our own name.

How quickly can you come to me?

We often have same-day availability. Call us before noon and we'll do our best to service your vehicle today.

Do you work with my insurance?

Yes. We bill insurance directly - no upfront cost to you. We'll even help verify your deductible.

What's included in the lifetime warranty?

Every windshield installation includes a lifetime warranty against defects and workmanship issues. It's better coverage than what most companies (including our previous employer) offer.

What is ADAS recalibration?

Modern vehicles have cameras and sensors in the windshield for safety features like lane departure warning and automatic braking. We recalibrate these systems after every installation. Many companies skip this critical step - we never do.

Should I repair or replace my windshield?
